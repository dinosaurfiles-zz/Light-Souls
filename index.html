How to Play.

The Soul of a Wizard is under the mercy of your hands. Guide him through his journey to reach Nirvana or let him suffer to his eternal damnation.
Don't let your enemies touch you.
Potions will be provided along the way.
Each level will be locked and a key should be found before you can proceed to the next stage
